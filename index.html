Welcome!

I am an incoming Assistant Professor in the Graduate School of Economics at Hitotsubashi University (starting in Fall 2024). For the 2023-24 academic year, I am a Specially Appointed Assistant Professor at Hitotsubashi Institute for Advanced Study (HIAS), Hitotsubashi University. I received my PhD in Economics and Public Policy from Tufts University in 2023.

My research interests are in environmental economics and development economics, with a focus on environmental pollution and health in developing countries. My work mainly draws on geospatial analysis of environmental data and field surveys in rural India.
